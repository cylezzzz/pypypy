📖 Installation Guide

1

Download Models

Click the download button on any model to get the files from Hugging Face or other repositories.

2

Place in Models Folder

Copy downloaded models to the appropriate subfolder in your models/ directory.

3

Scan & Refresh

Use the "Scan Directory" button to detect new models and make them available in the studio.

4

Start Creating

Select your new models in the Create Studio and start generating amazing AI content!
